This programm is an ML-model which is trained to recommend you clothes based on the style and similarities of your given input.

There are 3 ways to give your input to the programm (plus the gender choose). After you choosed an input, the programm will process your input. This can take a few seconds.

Your own image: use your own image of a clothing piece (the image will be saved!). We recommend to use a white or natural background and NEVER UPLOAD AN IMAGE WHERE THERE IS A PERSON IN.

With a Ready2Go image: pick an Ready2Go image to use. Click here for more info

With a product number: give the product number of your clothing piece. This is only available for a few stores. Click here for more info

With a product link: use the link of a product from a online shop. This is only available for a few stores. Click here for more info
